In total there is 30 teams in the NBA, 29 in USA and 1 in Canada. The one in Canada is in Toronto.The NBA wanted to expand into Canada. They started expansion teams in Toronto and Vancouver. The Vancouver franchise didn't work so they moved to Memphis. The Toronto team did and have stuck. The moves were part of making the NBA more international at the time. I suspect if they thought another Canadian city could sustain a team, they'd want to expand to that city, but there has been no indication that's of interest.
