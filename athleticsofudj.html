Athletics in U of D Jesuit

U of D Jesuit High School athletes compete in the Detroit Catholic High School League (CHSL) and the Michigan High School Athletic Association (MHSAA) while the Academy is a member of the Catholic Youth Organization (CYO). The department boasts 34 teams in total including 8 Academy teams and 14 varsity sports. Detroit Jesuit fields teams in fourteen sports: football, basketball, baseball, cross country, track and field, wrestling, tennis, golf, hockey, lacrosse, skiing, soccer, swimming, bowling.

Basketball

Basketball in UDJ

The varsity basketball team competes in the Detroit Catholic High School League (CHSL) and the Michigan High School Athletic Association (MHSAA). The program took home it's first state championship in 2016 with a win over North Farmington. The Cubs have won four straight Catholic League titles (13,14,15,16).

Head Coach

Mr. Patrick Donnelly
